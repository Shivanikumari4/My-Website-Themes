About Us

Our Daycare Centre thinks that children are capable of complicated thinking, are curious and have a lot of potential. Children are inherently motivated to learn and when they are exposed to enrich settings, nurturing adult interactions, support and the opportunity to explore. They can develop into self - motivated learners.

Children's understanding has grown over time, driven by ongoing study, experience and engagement with families and community partners. Our Centre's ability to adapt and adjust has enabled it to remain responsive and adaptable in terms of programme opportunities for children.


Our Philosophy

We believe that we must make each opportunity count. These are the foundational years for a happy and fulfilling life. A youngster learns more in the early years of learning rather than throughout their entire lifetime.

Our child care system is designed such that it entails in working on relevant and related tasks with other students collectively and in small groups, thereby allowing the child to grow emotionally and intellectually.
